1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Amity Drinks Influencer Monitor ("we", "our", "us") collects, uses, and protects data when using our internal influencer tracking application.

2. What Data We Collect

Our application collects only publicly available data from our own Instagram business account:

  • Instagram Business Account Data: Information from @amitydrinks.cz account
  • Tagged Posts and Stories: Content where our account is mentioned or tagged
  • Public Engagement Metrics: Likes, comments, shares, and reach data
  • Influencer Information: Public profile data of influencers who tag our account

3. How We Use This Data

We use the collected data exclusively for the following purposes:

  • Performance Tracking: Monitor influencer marketing campaign effectiveness
  • Internal Reports: Generate monthly performance reports for our marketing team
  • ROI Analysis: Calculate return on investment for influencer partnerships
  • Compensation: Determine influencer compensation based on reach and engagement

4. What Data We DON'T Collect

We explicitly do NOT collect:

  • Private user information or personal data
  • Direct messages or private communications
  • Data from accounts that don't interact with @amitydrinks.cz
  • Location data, device information, or browsing history
  • Any data beyond publicly available Instagram content

5. Data Storage and Security

  • Storage Location: Data is stored securely on EU-based servers
  • Access Control: Access restricted to authorized Amity Drinks employees only
  • Security Measures: Industry-standard encryption and security protocols
  • Retention: Data retained only as long as necessary for business purposes
  • Regular Audits: Regular security audits and compliance checks

6. Data Sharing

We do NOT share, sell, or distribute collected data to third parties. Data is used exclusively for internal Amity Drinks business operations.

7. Third-Party Services

Our application integrates with:

  • Meta Platform: Instagram Graph API for data collection
  • Compliance: We comply with Meta Platform Terms of Service and Data Policy

8. User Rights

If you are an influencer whose public Instagram data is tracked through our system, you have the right to:

  • Request information about what data we have collected
  • Request deletion of your data from our system
  • Opt-out of future tracking by not tagging @amitydrinks.cz

9. Cookies and Tracking

Our internal dashboard uses session cookies for authentication purposes only. We do not use tracking cookies or analytics beyond basic login functionality.

10. Compliance

  • GDPR: We comply with EU General Data Protection Regulation
  • Meta Terms: We adhere to Meta Platform Terms of Service
  • Data Protection: We follow industry best practices for data protection

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last Updated" date at the top of this page indicates when the policy was last revised.

12. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
